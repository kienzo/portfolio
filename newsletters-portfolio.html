AI-Powered Workflow

How Claude & Gemini accelerate editorial

A streamlined pipeline where AI handles the heavy lifting — from content generation to quality assurance — so editorial teams can focus on storytelling and strategy.

Claude
Content Drafting
Claude generates newsletter copy from editorial briefs — producing headlines, summaries, and body content tuned to brand voice and audience segments. Editors refine rather than create from scratch.
3× faster first drafts
Gemini
Subject Line Optimisation
Gemini generates and scores subject line variants using engagement data — predicting open rates and suggesting the highest-performing options for A/B testing in SFMC.
+18% open rate lift
Claude
Content QA & Compliance
Claude reviews final content for tone consistency, factual accuracy flags, link validation, and brand guideline compliance — catching issues before they reach millions of readers.
85% fewer revision cycles
SFMC
Dynamic Assembly
SFMC dynamically assembles personalised newsletter variants using AI-generated content blocks, audience segments, and Google Ad Manager placements — all automated at send time.
12+ content variants per send
Gemini
Performance Analysis
Gemini processes engagement data post-send — surfacing insights on content performance, segment behaviour, and trend signals that feed back into the next editorial cycle.
Real-time feedback loops
Claude
Analytics Narrative
Claude transforms raw dashboard metrics into plain-language reports for editorial and commercial stakeholders — making data accessible and actionable without analyst bottlenecks.
Weekly auto-generated briefs
AI Tool Stack

Claude & Gemini — different strengths, one pipeline

Each AI model is deployed where its capabilities are strongest — Claude for nuanced writing and reasoning, Gemini for data processing and multimodal analysis.

C

Claude

by Anthropic
Newsletter Copy Generation
Drafts newsletter sections from editorial briefs, maintaining brand voice consistency across different newsletter products and audience segments.
Content QA & Compliance
Reviews drafts for tone, accuracy, broken links, and adherence to editorial guidelines — reducing human review cycles by 85%.
Analytics Narratives
Converts complex dashboard data into weekly performance briefs that editorial and commercial teams can immediately act on.
Personalisation Logic
Helps design and iterate on recommendation engine rules — translating business requirements into content relevance scoring models.
G

Gemini

by Google
Subject Line Optimisation
Generates and scores hundreds of subject line variants, predicting open-rate performance to maximise SFMC A/B test effectiveness.
Engagement Data Analysis
Processes large-scale click, open, and conversion data — identifying content performance patterns and segment behaviour trends.
Image & Asset Review
Multimodal analysis of newsletter imagery for brand consistency, accessibility compliance, and visual quality before send.
Competitive Intelligence
Analyses competitor newsletter formats, frequency, and content strategies — surfacing opportunities for differentiation and growth.
