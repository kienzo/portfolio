Overview

The world's first 6DOF glasses

VLAVR was building the world's first consumer 6DOF (six degrees of freedom) AR/VR glasses — a lightweight, standalone headset with myopia adjustment and flexible temple design. The product targeted both consumer and enterprise markets, but needed a robust ecosystem of partners to reach commercial viability.

I joined to lead business development and project management, tasked with building the partnerships, ecosystem, and go-to-market strategy needed to take the hardware from prototype to global product.

Partnerships

Building the partner network

The VR glasses needed a full ecosystem to succeed — from chipsets and connectivity to content and peripherals. Securing the right partners in the right order was critical to market credibility and product viability.

Qualcomm

Partnered with Qualcomm to integrate their XR chipset platform, providing the processing power and reference design needed for a standalone 6DOF headset at consumer scale.

XR Chipset Hardware 5G Ready

Ericsson

Secured a partnership with Ericsson for 5G connectivity and cloud rendering — enabling low-latency streaming experiences and leading to a joint exhibition at Mobile World Congress 2020.
