03 — How It Works
From scattered inputs to structured output
1

Capture

Emails, documents, spreadsheets, and ad-hoc ideas arrive across seven connected platforms throughout the day.

2

Filter & Classify

GPT analyses each incoming item, tagging it by type, urgency, and relevance — routing actionable items forward and archiving noise.

3

Structure

Jira tickets are created with enriched context from Confluence. Ideas are logged into Jira Product Discovery. Data flows into Sheets for tracking.

4

Visualise

Roadmaps are generated from the unified data, tailored per stakeholder — executive summaries, engineering breakdowns, and product-centric views.

04 — Stakeholder Views
One source of truth, many lenses
👔

Executive Leadership

High-level strategic overview with business impact metrics, timeline milestones, and investment priorities — outcomes over implementation detail.

🛠️

Engineering Teams

Technical roadmap with Jira-linked epics, dependency mapping, capacity considerations, and sprint-level breakdowns ready for planning.

📈

Product Managers

Feature-centric view with user impact scoring, idea provenance tracking, and competitive context from aggregated discovery insights.

05 — Outcome
Turning operational chaos into clarity

These automations replaced a fragmented workflow where ideas, requests, and feedback were scattered across email threads, shared drives, and spreadsheets with no consistent path into planning.

By connecting seven platforms through Zapier with GPT-powered classification, the system creates a continuous pipeline from raw input to structured, stakeholder-ready roadmaps — eliminating manual triage and ensuring nothing falls through the cracks.

The result is a living system where every idea has a traceable path from capture to roadmap, and each stakeholder sees exactly the view they need to make decisions.
