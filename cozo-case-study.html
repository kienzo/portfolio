05 — Impact
Unified intelligence for safer spaces

The COZO Intelligence product suite replaced fragmented, siloed monitoring systems with a single AI-driven platform. Rather than each sensor and camera operating independently, building operators gained a unified view of safety and production across their entire facility.

By centralising IoT data through the Edge Smart Box and AI platform, COZO enabled real-time automated safety reporting — giving operators the high-level visibility needed to prevent incidents rather than just react to them.

The thermal camera solution proved especially impactful during the COVID-19 pandemic, enabling mass temperature screening in schools and public venues while simultaneously serving its core purpose of fire and overheating detection in industrial settings.
